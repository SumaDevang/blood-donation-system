Blood Donation Management System

Streamlining Blood Donation Processes for Donors, Hospitals, and Administrators

System Overview

The Blood Donation Management System is designed to enhance the coordination and efficiency of blood donation processes. It provides a centralized platform for managing donor information, hospital blood requests, and donation records, ensuring a steady supply of blood for medical needs.

Access by Role

  • Donors: Register as a donor, schedule donations, view your donation history, and manage upcoming appointments.
    Donate Blood
  • Hospitals: Manage blood requests, track donation schedules, and access donor information based on blood type requirements.
    Hospital Dashboard
  • Administrators: Oversee system operations, manage all data, and generate reports for blood donation activities.
    Admin Dashboard
