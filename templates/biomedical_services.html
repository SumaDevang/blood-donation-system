Our Biomedical Services

At the Blood Donation Management System, our biomedical services team works tirelessly to ensure that every unit of donated blood is safe, properly stored, and ready for use in life-saving medical procedures. Our services include:

  • Blood Testing: Every donation is rigorously tested for infectious diseases and blood type compatibility to ensure safety for recipients.
  • Storage and Preservation: Donated blood is stored under strict temperature-controlled conditions to maintain its viability for up to 42 days.
  • Distribution: We coordinate with hospitals to distribute blood units based on their specific needs, ensuring timely delivery for emergencies and scheduled procedures.
  • Quality Assurance: Our team follows strict regulatory guidelines to maintain the highest standards in blood processing and handling.
